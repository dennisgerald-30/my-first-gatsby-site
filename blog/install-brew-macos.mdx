### What is Brew?


Step 1: Open your terminal.

Step 2: Copy and paste the below command.

<CodeBlock>
 /bin/bash -c "$(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Homebrew/install/HEAD/install.sh)"
</CodeBlock>

Tips: You don't need to install SUDO as the script installs Homebrew to its preferred prefix.
