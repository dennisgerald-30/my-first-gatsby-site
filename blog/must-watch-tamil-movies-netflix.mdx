    Baahubali: The Beginning is a 2015 Indian epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli which was Shot simultaneously in Telugu and Tamil. The first of two cinematic parts, the film follows Shivudu / Shiva, an adventurous young man who helps his love Avanthika rescue Devasena, the former queen of Mahishmati who is now a prisoner under the tyrannical rule of king Bhallaladeva. The story concludes in Baahubali 2: The Conclusion. Critics praised the film for its direction, technical values, and the actors' performances.
</BlogCard>
